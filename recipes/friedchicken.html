Deep Fried Chicken

Description

Try this delicious cripsy juicy deep fried chicken recipe, great for couple's date night.

Ingredients

  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • Half teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Place crackers in a large resealable plastic bag; seal bag and crush crackers with rolling pin.
  2. Add the flour, potato flakes, seasoned salt, and pepper to resealable plastic bag then mix well.
  3. Beat egg in a shallow dish or bowl.
  4. One by one, dredge chicken pieces in egg, then place in bag with crumbs mixture. Seal bag and shake to coat.
  5. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  6. Fry chicken, turning frequently, until golden brown and juices run clear, 15 to 20 minutes.
