Cocunut Rice

Description

Not only does this creamy coconut rice takes under 30 minutes to make, but it's also filled with slightly sweet flavors that pair well with savory main dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut Milk
  • 1 pinch of salt.
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 cup of water

Steps

  1. Wash Rice to remove starch.
  2. Add coconut milk, water, sugar and salt in sauce pan and stir well for 2 minutes on medium heat.
  3. Add rice and bring to boil.
  4. Cover sauce pan and reduce heat to low.
  5. Let simmer until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed (15 to 20 minutes).
