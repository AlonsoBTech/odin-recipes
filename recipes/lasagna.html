Lasagna Stuffed Shell

Description

Lasagna stuffed shells are a great way to change up pasta night. I'm taking traditional lasagna and putting a twist on it by stuffing pasta shells with that amazing lasagna fillings.

If you're a fan of lasagna, you'll definetly want to give lasagna stuffed shells a try. I'm a huge lasagna lover, but never get around to making it because it always seems so tedious to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 18 jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 can (14 ounce) pasta sauce
  • 1 package shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups (16 ounce) ricotta cheese
  • 5 ounce fresh spinach
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 1 cup chopped bell pepper
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Boil Lasagna shell for 15 minutes on medium heat (Remember to add pinch of salt).
  2. Stir occasionally as to not let it stick.
  3. After lasagna shell is cooked drain the water then let sit in cold water for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour out the cold water after 5 minutes then set lasagna aside for later.
  5. Dice up garlic and bell pepper and place in large mixing bowl.
  6. Add as much spinach as desire to the large mixing bowl.
  7. Add ricotta cheese in large mixing bowl.
  8. Crack eggs into large mixing bowl and stir to mix all the ingredients together until thick.
  9. Cover bottom of baking pan with pasta sauce.
  10. Stuff each lasagna shell with ricotta mix and place in baking pan with pasta sauce.
  11. Add a spoonful of pasta sauce unto each lasagna shell then lightly cover with shredded cheese.
  12. Put to bake in oven for 15 minutes on 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) heat.
  13. Once finish let cool then serve.
